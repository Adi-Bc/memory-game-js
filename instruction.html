Welcome to Memory Game

Memory Game is a fun and challenging game where you test your memory skills by matching pairs of cards.

Instructions

  1. 😍Click on the "Start Game" button to begin the game.
  2. 😍Flip over two cards to reveal their images. Remember their positions.
  3. 😍If the images on the cards match, they will remain face up. If not, they will be flipped face down again.
  4. 😍Continue flipping cards and matching pairs until all pairs are found.
  5. 😍Once all pairs are found, you win the game!

Note: You can adjust the difficulty level by choosing different board sizes.
